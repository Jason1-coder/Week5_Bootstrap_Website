Chess Tactics to Master

Fork

A tactic where a single piece attacks two or more of the opponent's pieces simultaneously.

Pin

A tactic that immobilizes a piece because moving it would expose a more valuable piece behind it.

Skewer

A tactic where a more valuable piece is attacked, forcing it to move and exposing a less valuable piece behind it.

Discovered Attack

A tactic where one piece moves away, revealing an attack from another piece behind it.

Trapped Piece

A tactic where a piece is surrounded and cannot escape, leading to its capture.
